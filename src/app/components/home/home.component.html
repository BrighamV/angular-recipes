My pantry can help you save! With the help of My pantry you can find recipies that tailor to your fridge and pantry. Don't throw away the half onion you didnt use or the left over greek yogurt in your fridge. Use it! Just search for the ingredients you you need to use to find a recipe that fits your tastes. Give it a try and find hundreds of recipes that match your pantry.

Search With our search you can find recipes that fit your pantry. Here's the idea, lets say you cook something new for dinner one night. Because you try something new you have left over ingredients that you will likley let sit in your fridge untill it goes bad (we all do it). Instead of let the mold grow you can find a recipe that uses that ingredient. Resulting in less waste and more good food.
